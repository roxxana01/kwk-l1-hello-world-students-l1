Dr.Peyman and LASIK eye surgery

Dr.Peyman has won the Medal of Honor for LASIK eye surgery and continues to study at the University of Arizona.

"Dr. Peyman was born in Iran and as a young adult moved to Germany to study at the University of Freiburg where he received his M.D. in 1962. He completed his internship at St. Johannes Hospital in Diusberg, Germany in 1964 and at Passaic General Hospital in Passaic, New Jersey in 1965."- Arizona Retna Specialists.

"Gholam A. Peyman, MD is a Hall of Fame of Ophthalmology and retina surgeon who is also a prolific and successful inventor. Gholam Peyman has, thus far, been granted 150 US patents, covering a broad range of novel medical devices, intra-ocular drug delivery, surgical techniques, as well as new methods of diagnosis and treatment. His most widely-known invention to date is LASIKeye surgery, a revolutionary vision correction procedure which has enabled tens of millions of people to see clearly without glasses, for which he was awarded the first US patent in 1989. In addition to the numerous other honors and awards he has received, in 2005 he was selected by a ballot among the more than 30,000 ophthalmologists around the world to become one of the thirteen living ophthalmologists inducted into the Hall of Fame of Ophthalmology."- University of Arizona College of Medicine .


WHAT IS LASIK EYE SURGERY ???

LASIK eye surgery is a vision correction procedure, that uses a laser to resolve myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism, to allow people to see clearly without glasses or contacts .
